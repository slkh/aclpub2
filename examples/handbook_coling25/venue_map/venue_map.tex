 {\Large \textbf{The Convention Centre}}\\\leavevmode\newline
\noindent{COLING 2025 is held in the Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre (ADNEC).}\\

\noindent{The Registration Desk (January 18 to January 24, 2025 ) is in The Link (next to Hall B).}\\

\noindent{Workshops and tutorials (January 19 \& 20, 2025) are in Capital Suites 5 to 10, 13. Halls B-A, B-B, B-C, B-D.}\\

\noindent{Coffee breaks for the Main Conference are held in the Link and Atrium. Coffee breaks for Workshop are held in the walkways outside the capital suites and Hall B.}\\

\noindent{The Main Conference (January  21 to 24, 2025) is held in Hall A (Plenary), Hall B Rooms A-D, Capital Suite 7 (Oral Group Discussions). Group Discussions for Demos, posters, workshops along with exhibitions, will be located in The Link and The Atrium areas, between and adjacent to Halls A and B.}\\
